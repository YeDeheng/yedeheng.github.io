Short Bio: I am Deheng Ye (叶德珩), currently a visiting fellow at Microsoft Research Asia. I did my Ph.D. at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My advisers are Dr. Zhenchang Xing, Dr. Nachiket Kapre, and Dr. Shang-Wei Lin. I got my B.Eng. degree from the Gifted Class of Northwestern Polytechnical University, China. My research interests lie in software analytics, i.e., data mining in software engineering. I also did some work in parallel computing using GPUs and reconfigurable computing using FPGAs.
